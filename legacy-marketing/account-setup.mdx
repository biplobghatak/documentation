Before Spyro can start tracking your AI citations and generating content, you need to create an account, activate your free trial, and complete a few one-time configuration steps. This page covers everything from signing up to securing your account - so you can move on to the parts that actually grow your visibility.

## Creating Your Account

Go to [spyro.app/signup](https://spyro.app/signup) and choose one of two sign-up methods:

- **Email and password** - Enter your work email and choose a strong password. Spyro will send a confirmation link to verify your address.
- **Google OAuth** - Click **Continue with Google** to authenticate instantly with your Google account. No separate password needed.

Both methods give you identical access to every feature. If you use Google OAuth, your account is secured by your Google credentials and two-factor authentication settings at the Google level.

<Note>
  Your **3-day free trial** begins the moment you create your account. The trial includes up to **3 blog article generations** at no cost so you can experience the full content pipeline. **Your trial does not auto-charge** - when it ends, your account moves to a read-only state and no payment is taken until you actively choose to upgrade. Your data is preserved if you upgrade later.
</Note>

## Setting Up Your Account and Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email Address">
    After signing up with email and password, check your inbox for a verification email from **hello@spyro.app** and click the confirmation link. Google OAuth accounts are verified automatically.

    You must verify your email before Spyro can run your initial site audit or send you citation reports.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your Domain">
    When you first log in, Spyro prompts you to create a workspace. Enter your full domain URL - for example, `https://yourcompany.com` - and give the workspace a recognizable name.

    A **workspace** is Spyro's container for a single website. All citation data, competitor tracking, content queues, and CMS connections are scoped to the workspace they belong to.

    Your **Pro plan includes up to 2 workspaces**, which means you can monitor two separate domains from one account - ideal if you run multiple brands or manage a small number of client sites.

    <Info>
      Each workspace runs its own independent audit, citation tracking, and content pipeline. Switching between workspaces takes one click from the top navigation bar.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Google Search Console">
    Connecting Google Search Console gives Spyro access to your real organic search performance data. Spyro uses this to cross-reference your traditional SEO signals with your AI citation data, giving you a more complete picture of your content gaps.

    To connect:

    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** inside your workspace.
    2. Click **Connect** next to Google Search Console.
    3. Authenticate with the Google account that has access to your Search Console property.
    4. Select the property that matches your workspace domain and click **Confirm**.

    <Tip>
      If your domain isn't verified in Google Search Console yet, you can skip this step for now and come back later. Spyro's AI citation tracking works independently of Search Console - the connection is optional but recommended.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Target Keywords and Competitors">
    Spyro automatically infers your niche and an initial competitor list from your domain during the first audit. After the audit completes, you can refine both.

    Navigate to **Workspace Settings → Targeting** to:

    - Add or remove **target keywords** - the topics you most want to earn AI citations for
    - Edit your **competitor list** - the domains Spyro benchmarks your citation rate against
    - Set your **content language and region** if you target a specific locale

    The topical map Spyro builds for your content calendar is generated from these inputs, so spending a few minutes here pays off across every article Spyro writes for you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Plans and Trial Details

<Accordion title="What's included in the free trial?">
  Your 3-day free trial includes:

  - Full access to the Spyro dashboard and AI Visibility Score
  - Up to **3 blog article generations** from the GEO content engine
  - 1 workspace with a full initial site audit
  - Citation tracking across all five AI engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot)

  The trial does not require a credit card and does not auto-charge when it ends.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What does the Pro plan include?">
  The **Pro + Done For You** plan is priced at **$99/month** (billed monthly) or **$990/year** (billed annually - 2 months free). The regular price is $149/month. A **14-day money-back guarantee** applies to all paid subscriptions.

  The plan includes:

  - Up to **2 workspaces** (2 separate domains)
  - **30 SEO/GEO-Optimized Articles per month** (1 per day) at 2,500–3,000 words each
  - Auto Internal & External Linking on every article
  - AI Mentions Tracking across all five engines
  - Auto AI Images included with each article
  - Autopilot publishing to WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, and Wix
  - **Unlimited Team Members**
  - **Priority Support**
  - Full access to APIs and webhooks
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I upgrade after my trial?">
  Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade to Pro**. You'll be prompted to enter a payment method, and your subscription begins immediately. Your trial data - including your initial audit results and any generated articles - carries over automatically.
</Accordion>

## Managing Your Account

### Profile and Preferences

Access your profile settings at any time by navigating to **Settings → Account**. From here you can:

- Update your display name and email address
- Change your password (email/password accounts only)
- Set your preferred timezone for content scheduling and citation reports
- Manage notification preferences for daily citation digests and article generation alerts

### Billing and Subscription

Navigate to **Settings → Billing** to view your current plan, upcoming invoice, and payment method. You can update your card, download past invoices, or upgrade your plan from this screen.

**To cancel your subscription**, click **Cancel Plan** in **Settings → Billing**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current paid billing period - you retain full access until then and your data is preserved for 30 days after the period ends.

<Warning>
  Cancelling your plan stops all article generation and autopilot publishing immediately at the end of the billing period. Citation tracking and dashboard access also pause. Export any content you want to keep before your period ends.
</Warning>

## Security and Data Privacy

Spyro is built with security defaults that protect your data without requiring any configuration on your part.

| Protection | Details |
|---|---|
| **Passwords** | Hashed using bcrypt - Spyro staff cannot read your password |
| **Data in transit** | Encrypted with TLS 1.2 or higher on all connections |
| **Data at rest** | Encrypted at the storage layer |
| **Row-level security** | Your workspace data is isolated - other users cannot access it |

For questions about data handling or compliance, contact [hello@spyro.app](mailto:hello@spyro.app).
