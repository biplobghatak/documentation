## Free trial

Spyro offers a **3-day free trial** so you can explore the platform before committing. During the trial you can generate up to 3 blog articles and see how citation monitoring works on your domain.

<Info>
  The trial does **not** auto-charge you when it ends. You only start paying if you actively add a payment method. There's no card required to begin the trial — just sign up at [spyro.app/signup](https://spyro.app/signup).
</Info>

## 14-day money-back guarantee

If you start a paid plan and decide Spyro isn't the right fit, you can request a full refund within **14 days of your first charge**. Email [support@spyro.app](mailto:support@spyro.app) with your account email and a brief note, and the team will process your refund promptly — no lengthy questionnaires required.

## How billing works

Spyro's billing is processed by **Dodo Payments**, who acts as the Merchant of Record. This means Dodo Payments handles all sales tax, GST, and VAT calculations and remittance on your behalf — you don't need to worry about regional tax compliance.

- **Monthly plans** renew automatically on the same calendar date each month.
- **Annual plans** are billed upfront in full at the time of purchase and renew after 12 months.
- You'll receive an emailed receipt after every successful charge.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, Spyro retries the charge automatically over the following days. You'll receive an email notification prompting you to update your payment method in **Settings → Billing**.

<Warning>
  If a payment remains unresolved after the retry period, your account will become **read-only**. You'll still be able to view your data, but article generation, auto-publishing, and other active features will be paused until a successful payment is processed.
</Warning>

## Cancelling your subscription

To cancel, go to **Settings → Billing** inside the app and follow the cancellation prompts. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current paid period — you keep full access to all features until then. There are no long-term contracts and no cancellation fees.

<Tip>
  Before cancelling, consider exporting your content and citation data from the dashboard so you have a local copy for your records.
</Tip>
