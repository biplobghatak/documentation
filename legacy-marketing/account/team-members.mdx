The Pro plan includes **unlimited team members**, so you can bring your entire content team, SEO specialists, or clients into Spyro without paying extra per seat. Each person gets their own login and access to the specific workspace you invite them to - nothing more, nothing less.

<Warning>
  Never share your login credentials with colleagues or clients. Shared logins make it impossible to audit who made changes and can put your account security at risk. Always invite teammates through the app so each person has their own account.
</Warning>

## How to invite a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your workspace settings">
    Log in to [app.spyro.app](https://app.spyro.app) and make sure you're in the correct workspace. Click **Settings** in the left-hand navigation, then select **Team**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the team member's email address">
    Click **Invite Team Member**, type in their email address, and click **Send Invite**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Team member accepts the invitation">
    Your teammate will receive an invitation email from Spyro. If they already have a Spyro account, they can log in and accept. If they're new, they'll be prompted to create an account first. Once accepted, they'll have access to the workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Every team member must be **18 years of age or older** to use Spyro in accordance with our Terms of Service. Please ensure any person you invite meets this requirement before sending an invitation.
</Note>

## What team members can access

Team membership is scoped to the workspace you invite them to. A team member added to your `yoursite.com` workspace can see and interact with that workspace's keywords, citations, content queue, and integrations - but they cannot see data from any other workspace on your account unless you invite them there separately.

## Removing a team member

To revoke a team member's access, go to **Settings → Team**, find their name in the list, and click **Remove**. Their access is revoked immediately. They will not receive a notification email, so communicate any access changes to your team directly.

## Agency and client collaboration

If you're an agency, you can invite clients as team members inside their respective workspace. This gives clients visibility into their own citation data and content queue without exposing other client workspaces. You stay in control - only workspace admins can invite or remove members.
