A workspace in Spyro is a self-contained environment for a single website or domain. Everything Spyro tracks and generates for that site - keywords, citation monitoring, content queue, audits, and CMS integrations - lives inside its workspace. If you manage more than one site, each site gets its own workspace so data never mixes between them.

## What's included in each workspace

Every workspace gives you the full Spyro feature set scoped to one domain:

## How to create a workspace

When you first sign up, Spyro walks you through creating your initial workspace. To add a second workspace later, follow these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Spyro">
    Go to [app.spyro.app](https://app.spyro.app) and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the workspace selector">
    Click the workspace name in the top-left corner of the dashboard to open the workspace dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Workspace or Add Site">
    Select **New Workspace** (or **Add Site**) from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your domain URL">
    Type in the full domain you want to track, for example `https://yoursite.com`. Click **Continue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the initial audit">
    Spyro automatically runs a site crawl, maps your competitors, and builds a 30-day topical content plan. This typically completes within a few minutes. You'll see a progress indicator in the dashboard - your citation data will appear within 24 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The **Pro plan includes up to 2 workspaces**. If you need more than 2 - for example, if you're an agency managing multiple client sites - contact us at [support@spyro.app](mailto:support@spyro.app) to discuss volume pricing. See [Plans & Billing](/account/plans-billing) for full details on agency discounts.
</Note>

## Switching between workspaces

To move between your workspaces, click the workspace name in the top-left corner of the dashboard and select the site you want to work on from the dropdown. Each workspace is fully independent - switching workspaces loads a completely fresh set of data for that domain.
