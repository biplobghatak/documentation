About Spyro

# We make the invisible half of search visible.

We’re Spyro, the AI-visibility and SEO platform built for founders like you. See exactly where you stand in ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and on Google, then let us handle the hard part on autopilot.

[Our Story](https://spyro.app/#our-story)

Our story

## It started with traffic we couldn’t explain.

We were the customer before we were the company.

We're founders and bloggers. We did the work: keyword research, audits, long posts, and watched our Google rankings like everyone else.

Then people started showing up already knowing us. “ChatGPT recommended you.” Sometimes an answer engine sent them our way; sometimes it sent them to a competitor instead. We had no way to see which, how often, or why.

So we tracked it by hand: spreadsheets of prompts, screenshots of answers, notes on which posts got quoted. The pattern was obvious and a little unsettling. A whole new layer of discovery had appeared, and almost no one could measure it.

Spyro is the tool we wished existed that week: one place to rank on Google and to see and shape how AI talks about you.

Why we exist

## Search didn’t die. 
It split in two.

Half your audience still Googles. The other half asks an AI, and only hears the brands it decides to name.

For twenty years, being found meant ranking on a page of blue links. That still matters, and Spyro still does it.

But more and more, people skip the links. They ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, or Gemini and get one answer naming a few brands. If you're not one of them, there's no second-place link to click.

The catch: the SEO tools everyone relies on can't see any of this. They were built for a web that's only half the story now.

That blind spot is the whole reason Spyro exists.

Our mission

## Make every founder visible on Google and inside AI, without a specialist, an agency, or a five-figure budget.

Enterprise-grade visibility shouldn’t require an enterprise budget. We built Spyro so a team of one can stand shoulder to shoulder with a team of twenty: no specialist, no agency, no stress.

Product philosophy

## We think measurement should come before opinions.

Most “AI SEO” advice is vibes. We built a loop instead.

You can’t optimize what you can’t see, and you can’t scale what you do by hand. So Spyro insists on order: measure your standing across every engine, audit what’s missing, create content structured the way answer engines reward, publish it automatically, then measure again. The product isn’t any one feature; it’s the loop, built so a non-specialist can run all of it.

> Measure before you optimize. Automate before you scale.
