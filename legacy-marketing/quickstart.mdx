Getting from zero to your first AI Visibility Score takes less than 15 minutes. This guide walks you through every step - from creating your account to reviewing live citation data across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot. Follow the steps below in order and Spyro's autopilot pipeline will take care of everything else.

<Note>
  Your first AI citation data appears within **24 hours** of completing your initial audit. You don't need to do anything extra - Spyro queries all five AI engines automatically and populates your dashboard overnight.
</Note>

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Head to [spyro.app/signup](https://spyro.app/signup) and register with your email address and a password, or sign in with Google OAuth for one-click access.

    Your account starts on a **3-day free trial** - no credit card is required to sign up. The trial includes up to three blog article generations so you can see the full content pipeline before committing.

    Once you confirm your email address, you'll land in the Spyro dashboard and be prompted to create your first workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Workspace">
    A **workspace** represents a single website or domain. Everything in Spyro - your citation data, content calendar, competitor tracking, and CMS connection - is organized inside a workspace.

    To create yours:

    1. Click **Create Workspace** on the welcome screen.
    2. Enter your domain URL (e.g., `https://yourcompany.com`).
    3. Give the workspace a name that's easy to recognize - especially useful if you manage multiple clients.
    4. Click **Save** to continue.

    Your Pro plan includes up to **2 workspaces**, so you can track two separate domains from the same account.

    <Tip>
      If you're an agency managing client sites, create a separate workspace for each client domain. This keeps citation data, content pipelines, and CMS connections completely isolated per client.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run the Initial Audit">
    With your domain saved, Spyro immediately begins the initial audit. This is a one-time crawl that does three things:

    - **Crawls your site** to catalog your existing content and identify structural gaps
    - **Maps your competitors** by analyzing which domains earn AI citations on topics relevant to your niche
    - **Builds a 30-day topical map** - a prioritized list of article topics designed to maximize your citation rate

    The audit typically completes in a few minutes. You'll see a progress indicator in your workspace dashboard, and you'll receive an email notification when it's done.

    <Info>
      You don't need to configure anything for the initial audit to run. Spyro infers your niche and competitors automatically from your domain. You can refine your competitor list and target keywords afterward in **Workspace Settings**.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your CMS">
    Connecting your CMS lets Spyro publish articles directly to your site on a schedule - no copy-pasting or manual uploads required. Supported platforms include WordPress.org, WordPress.com, Shopify, Webflow, and Wix.

    Navigate to **Settings → Integrations** inside your workspace and choose your platform:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card
        title="WordPress"
        icon="wordpress"
        href="/integrations/wordpress"
      >
        Supports both WordPress.org (self-hosted) and WordPress.com. Connect via API key or OAuth.
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="Shopify"
        icon="bag-shopping"
        href="/integrations/shopify"
      >
        Publish blog posts directly to your Shopify store's blog using the Shopify Admin API.
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="Webflow"
        icon="globe"
        href="/integrations/webflow"
      >
        Connect Webflow via API token to publish to any CMS collection on your Webflow site.
      </Card>
      <Card
        title="APIs & Webhooks"
        icon="code"
        href="/integrations/api-webhooks"
      >
        Use Spyro's REST API or outbound webhooks to push content to any custom platform or Wix site.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Tip>
      You can still use Spyro without a CMS connection. Articles will accumulate in your **Content Queue** where you can review, edit, and export them manually at any time.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Your AI Visibility Score">
    Once the initial audit is complete and the first overnight citation sweep has run, open your workspace dashboard to see your **AI Visibility Score**.

    The score reflects how often your domain is cited - versus your competitors - when AI engines answer questions in your topic area. It's expressed as a percentage and broken down by:

    - **AI engine** (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot)
    - **Topic cluster** (based on the topical map Spyro built during your audit)
    - **Competitor comparison** (how your citation rate stacks up against the domains competing for the same queries)

    Use this score as your north-star metric. Spyro's content pipeline is designed to move it upward over time - one article per day, every day.

    <Tip>
      Invite your teammates to the workspace so they can follow progress alongside you. Go to **Settings → Team**, enter their email addresses, and assign them either an Admin or Viewer role. Teammates can be added from any screen inside the app.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens Next

After setup, Spyro runs its citation-tracking and content-generation pipeline automatically. Each day it:

1. Queries all five AI engines and records updated citation data
2. Refreshes your AI Visibility Score and competitor comparisons
3. Generates the next article from your topical map
4. Publishes it to your connected CMS (if autopilot is enabled)

You can let the pipeline run hands-free or drop into the dashboard at any time to approve articles before they publish, adjust your topical map, or update your competitor list.
