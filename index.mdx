## How Spyro Works

Spyro runs a continuous loop that compounds your visibility over time: measure your AI and Google standing, audit what's missing, create citation-worthy content, publish it automatically, and measure again.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Audit & Setup">
    Spyro crawls your site, connects your CMS, and establishes your baseline AI citation and SEO standing across all major engines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gap Discovery">
    Competitor analysis surfaces every topic where rivals earn AI citations and Google rankings that you don't — your prioritised opportunity list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Silo Mapping">
    Spyro builds a 30-day topical authority map, grouping content into tightly structured silos that signal expertise to both AI engines and Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="GEO Content">
    The built-in content engine produces answer-first, schema-rich articles of 2,500–3,000 words structured to be cited by AI engines and rank on Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Asset Optimization">
    Existing pages are enhanced with structured data, internal links, and citation-ready formatting so your current content works harder immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Autopilot Sync">
    Daily CMS sync pushes finished articles directly to your WordPress, Shopify, or Webflow site — no copy-paste required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auditing & Verification">
    Weekly AI visibility audits confirm new citations are appearing across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot as expected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Indexing Audits">
    450 daily index checks catch crawl issues, indexing gaps, and technical blockers before they cost you rankings or citations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
