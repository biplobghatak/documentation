More than half of all search activity now flows through AI engines — and traditional SEO tools can't see any of it. Spyro is an AI-powered SEO and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) platform built for this new reality. It monitors when and where your brand gets cited across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot, identifies the content gaps that are costing you visibility, generates publication-ready articles optimized for AI search, and publishes them directly to your CMS — all on autopilot.

## The Problem With Traditional SEO Tools

Search behavior has fundamentally shifted. When someone asks an AI assistant a question about your industry, product, or competitors, the frequency with which your brand is cited — versus your competitors — determines your AI visibility. Rank trackers, keyword tools, and backlink auditors were built for the old web — they have no visibility into AI-generated responses, which means you're flying blind in the channel that is growing fastest.

Spyro closes that gap. It runs daily queries across every major AI engine, captures citation data in real time, and translates those signals into a clear action plan.

## How Spyro Works

Spyro operates as a continuous five-stage loop so your AI visibility compounds over time rather than stagnating after a single audit.

| Stage | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **Measure** | Spyro queries AI engines daily and records how often your brand and competitors are cited across each engine |
| **Audit** | It maps those citations against your existing content to surface gaps and missed opportunities |
| **Create** | The content engine drafts GEO-optimized articles (2,500–3,000 words) targeted at your gaps |
| **Publish** | Articles are pushed directly to your connected CMS — WordPress, Shopify, or Webflow |
| **Re-measure** | New citation data flows in within 24 hours, and the loop begins again |

## The 8-Step Pipeline

Behind that loop is a structured eight-step pipeline that covers everything from your first site crawl to ongoing indexing audits:

1. **Audit & Setup** — Crawl your domain and establish your baseline AI Visibility Score
2. **Gap Discovery** — Identify topics where competitors earn AI citations and you don't
3. **Silo Mapping** — Organize your content into topical clusters that AI engines reward
4. **GEO Content** — Generate articles written specifically to be cited by AI responses
5. **Asset Optimization** — Improve existing pages to increase their citation potential
6. **Autopilot Sync** — Schedule and publish content to your CMS automatically
7. **Auditing & Verification** — Confirm published articles are indexed and performing
8. **Indexing Audits** — Detect and resolve crawl or indexing issues that suppress visibility
