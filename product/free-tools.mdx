Spyro publishes five **free public tools** at
[spyro.app/free-tools](https://www.spyro.app/free-tools). They run without an
account, an email gate, or a credit card - they're the same audit and analysis
engines that power the app, opened up for anyone. Each one analyzes a domain you
type in and gives you a score plus a prioritized list of fixes.

<Note>
These tools are **public marketing pages**, not part of the signed-in app. They're
a no-risk way to see what Spyro finds before you create a workspace.
</Note>

## SEO & GEO Audit

[**/free-tools/seo-geo-audit**](https://www.spyro.app/free-tools/seo-geo-audit) -
one scan that grades both your traditional SEO health and your **GEO readiness**:
whether ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity can find and cite you.

- **What you check** - enter your domain and Spyro crawls your site.
- **What you get** - separate **SEO** and **GEO** readiness scores, the full list
  of issues found, and prioritized fixes ranked by impact.

<Tip>
Spyro serves one fresh audit per site every 24 hours. Re-running a domain within
that window shows the cached report - and offers to email you free weekly updates.
</Tip>

## AI Visibility & Citation Readiness Checker

[**/free-tools/ai-visibility-checker**](https://www.spyro.app/free-tools/ai-visibility-checker) - a crawl-based score of how prepared your site is to be **cited** by AI search
and answer engines.

- **What you check** - Spyro discovers your sitemap and crawls up to 20 pages of
  your public, server-rendered HTML, then extracts structured data, headings,
  content format, internal links, and crawlability.
- **What you get** - an overall readiness score across **six signals** - Citation
  Readiness, Entity Clarity, Content Coverage, GEO Readiness, Knowledge Signals,
  and Citation Authority - plus your strengths, your gaps, and priority actions.

## AI Crawler & robots.txt Checker

[**/free-tools/ai-crawler-robots-checker**](https://www.spyro.app/free-tools/ai-crawler-robots-checker) - confirms that AI crawlers are actually allowed to read your site. If they can't
crawl you, they can't cite you.

- **What you check** - Spyro fetches your `robots.txt`, `sitemap`, and `llms.txt`
  directly (no full-site crawl) and resolves the exact rule that allows or blocks
  each AI user-agent: **GPTBot**, **ClaudeBot**, **PerplexityBot**, **Googlebot**,
  and more. It also flags **silent blocks** - engines your server or WAF blocks
  even when `robots.txt` allows them.
- **What you get** - an accessibility score, a per-engine list of who can cite you,
  and a copy-paste `robots.txt` that opens you up to AI answer engines.

## Schema Markup Validator

[**/free-tools/schema-markup-validator**](https://www.spyro.app/free-tools/schema-markup-validator) - validates your JSON-LD structured data **site-wide** against schema.org and
Google's rich-result rules.

- **What you check** - Spyro crawls up to 20 pages, parses every JSON-LD block,
  resolves `@graph` and `@id` references, groups the data by schema type, and
  checks each type's required and recommended properties, dates, URLs, and
  structure.
- **What you get** - a schema-health score, a per-type breakdown with the exact
  errors and warnings, and priority fixes ranked by impact.

## Meta & Snippet Preview

[**/free-tools/meta-snippet-preview**](https://www.spyro.app/free-tools/meta-snippet-preview) - preview how your title and meta description render in Google, measured in
**pixels** rather than character counts.

- **What you check** - type a title and description, or paste a URL to pull a live
  page's existing tags. Everything runs locally in your browser - no crawl, no AI
  tokens, no data sent anywhere (except the optional "fetch from URL" lookup).
- **What you get** - a live desktop and mobile SERP preview (light and dark),
  pixel meters that estimate where Google truncates, a 0-100 snippet score, and
  copy-ready meta tags.

## Saving and sharing your report

Three of the crawl-based tools - the **SEO & GEO Audit**, **AI Visibility
Checker**, and **Schema Validator** (and the **AI Crawler Checker**) - let you
**download a PDF** of your report:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Download PDF">
    A lightweight modal opens, offering free weekly SEO & GEO updates for your
    domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email - or skip">
    Add your email to subscribe, or choose **Skip and download**. Either way the
    PDF generates and downloads automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The email is entirely **optional** - it signs you up for updates, and the report
itself is never gated behind it.
