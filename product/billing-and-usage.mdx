Spyro keeps pricing simple: try it free, then pick a plan based on how many
websites you run. This page explains the plans, what's included, and how to manage
it all.

## Start with a free trial

New accounts begin with a **3-day free trial** that lets you create **3 articles**
total - enough to take the writer for a spin. When the trial ends, you'll choose a
plan to keep going.

### Agency discounts

The price per website drops the more sites you run:

| Websites | You save | Roughly per site |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 3-5 | 10% off | ~$89/site/month |
| 6-10 | 15% off | ~$84/site/month |
| 11-20 | 20% off | ~$79/site/month |
| 21+ | Custom pricing | [Talk to us](mailto:hello@spyro.app) |

Running 21 or more websites? Reach out at `hello@spyro.app` for a custom plan.

<Tip>
Pay **yearly** and you get two months free - you pay for 10 months instead of 12.
</Tip>

## What you get

Every website on either plan comes with the same generous monthly allowances:

| What you get | How much |
| --- | --- |
| Articles written | **30 / month** |
| AI credits (chat + keyword research) | **200 / month** |
| Keywords you can track | up to **500** |
| AI assistants tracked | **All of them** - Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity |
| Connected tools | **5** per website |
| Images per article | 3 |
| Competitor comparison | Included |
| Team members | **Unlimited** |

The one extra in **Agency** is white-labeling - putting your own brand on the
reports.

## Where to see your usage

Open the **Usage** page from your account settings to see how much you've used this
month. You'll find a progress bar for each thing you can use up - articles, AI
credits, and more - so you always know where you stand. Everything resets at the
start of each month.

<Note>
During your trial, the articles bar shows your 3-article trial allowance instead of
the monthly plan figure.
</Note>

## Upgrade or change your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Plan & billing">
    Go to your account settings and open **Plan & billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a plan">
    Choose Pro or Agency, monthly or yearly, and - for Agency - how many websites.
    Spyro opens a secure checkout for you to pay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change it any time">
    Already subscribed? Adjust your plan, billing cycle, or number of websites and
    Spyro updates it on the spot. To go smaller, remove any extra websites first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage your billing">
    Use **Manage billing** to update your card, download invoices, and review your
    subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Billing covers your whole account, not a single website. Only the account owner can
change the plan or payment details.
</Note>
