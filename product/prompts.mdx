A **tracking prompt** is a real buyer question - the kind a customer would actually
type into an AI assistant, like *"best budget standing desk for a home office."*
Every week, Spyro asks the AI assistants your prompts and records how often the
answers mention you.

Prompts are the starting point for [AI visibility](/product/ai-visibility): the
questions you set here are what everything else measures. The trick is to ask the
way a customer would - never your own brand name - because the whole point is to
see whether AI recommends you on its own.

## You start with suggestions

You don't begin with a blank page. When you first set up Spyro during
[onboarding](/product/onboarding), it suggests a starter set of buyer questions
based on your website and competitors. They cover a natural mix - recommendations,
comparisons, and how-to questions - and you can review or edit them before they go
live.

## How to add and manage prompts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Tracking prompts">
    In the sidebar, open **Tracking prompts** (part of AI visibility).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a buyer question">
    Use the **Add prompt** box and phrase it the way a real customer would - for
    example, `best budget standing desk for a home office`. Don't include your own
    brand name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the AI assistants">
    Tick which assistants should answer this question - **ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude,
    Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews**. They're all selected by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track it">
    Click **Track prompt**. It runs once right away so you get a first result, then
    re-checks automatically every week.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To change a question later, edit it from its card. You can **pause** a prompt to
stop checking it, or **delete** it to remove it for good. (Deleting also erases
that prompt's history, so pause instead if you might want it back.)

## Reading your results

Each prompt appears as a card showing how it's doing this week:

- **How often you're mentioned** - your mention rate for that question this week.
- **The trend** - whether you're up or down compared with last week.
- **Per-assistant results** - how you did on each AI assistant you picked.
- **Competitor sites** - the rival domains the assistants mentioned for this
  question. This is a quick way to see who you're competing against.

Open any prompt to see its full history - how each assistant's results have moved
over time, your top competitor for that question, and the most recent answers that
mentioned you.

## What to expect

- Results are shown as a frequency, like **"mentioned in 3 of 5 checks,"** because
  AI answers vary each time.
- Your first result appears the moment you add a prompt; after that, Spyro
  re-checks every week automatically.
- The trend needs a couple of weeks of data before it means much.

## Tips

- Keep prompts in your customer's words. If it sounds like an SEO keyword, rewrite
  it as a natural question.
- Use the competitor sites on each card to spot rivals worth studying over on
  [AI visibility](/product/ai-visibility).
- To read the exact wording of an answer, jump to [AI mentions](/product/ai-mentions).
