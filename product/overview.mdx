Spyro is your all-in-one tool for getting found online - both on **Google** and
inside **AI assistants** like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude.

You don't need to be technical to use it. Spyro checks your website, tracks your
rankings, tells you whether AI tools mention your brand, and even writes and
publishes content for you. This guide explains every feature in plain language,
step by step.

## Who Spyro is for

Spyro is built for people who want more traffic and visibility, without the
technical headache:

- SEO professionals and agencies
- Marketing teams
- Content writers
- Website and business owners

If you can use a website, you can use Spyro.

## How Spyro is organized

A quick bit of vocabulary that makes everything else easier:

- A **workspace** is one website you want to grow. Everything - your audits,
  rankings, content, and settings - belongs to a workspace.
- If you manage more than one website, you can have a workspace for each one and
  switch between them at the top of the screen.

That's it. Most of the time you'll work inside a single workspace for your site.
