**Spyro AI research** is a chat agent that does the legwork of SEO and content
research for you. Ask it a question in plain language and it picks the right tools
— keyword discovery, SERP analysis, content-gap finding, your own indexed site,
Google Search Console, the rank tracker — and answers with real data, not guesses.
It's at `/{org}/{workspace}/spyro-ai-agent` (**Spyro AI research** in the sidebar).

<Note>
The agent is **locale-aware**: it uses your workspace's target country and content
language, so keyword volumes and topic ideas reflect the market you're targeting.
Set these in [workspace settings](/product/settings).
</Note>

## What you can ask

The agent has a large toolset (keyword research, SERP and competitor analysis,
content-gap and rankable-topic finding, GSC overview/queries/low-CTR/almost-ranking/
content-decay, internal-link suggestions, your content schedule, and article
drafting). Some examples:

- "Find me ten low-difficulty keywords about *cold brew coffee*."
- "What content gaps do my competitors cover that I don't?"
- "Which of my pages are almost ranking — page 1, positions 8–20?"
- "Suggest blog topics for next month and add them to my calendar."
- "Draft an article for *best travel backpacks*."

<Tip>
When you open the page, Spyro shows **suggested prompts** tailored to your
workspace — based on your current SEO/GEO scores, top audit issues, Search Console
opportunities, and seed keywords. They're a good starting point.
</Tip>

## Using the chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a conversation">
    Type a question, or click a suggested prompt. Each visit opens a **fresh
    chat** — previous threads aren't auto-resumed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch it use tools">
    The agent streams its reasoning and the tools it runs (e.g. a keyword lookup or
    a SERP scan), then summarizes the result. Tool results render inline — for
    example, recommended blog ideas appear as cards you can send to the writer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rate the answer">
    Use the **thumbs up / down** on any response. Your feedback is saved per
    message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revisit past chats">
    Open **Chats** (top-right) to reopen any previous thread for this workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Credits

The agent draws from your workspace's shared **AI-credit pool** (the
`AI credits this month` counter at the top of the page). The same pool powers
keyword-research searches. Limits come with your plan — see
[Plans, billing & usage](/product/billing-and-usage).

## Demo mode and advanced search

If no chat LLM key is configured, the page shows a **demo-mode** banner and the
agent returns sample answers. The **Advanced search** page
(`/{org}/{workspace}/spyro-ai-agent/search`) is a non-chat keyword-research tool
that still works directly — it's no longer in the sidebar but remains reachable by
URL.
