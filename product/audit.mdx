An **audit** is a full health check of your website. Spyro looks over your whole
site and scores it on two things: classic **SEO** (how well Google can find,
read, and rank your pages) and **AI readiness** (how easily AI assistants like
ChatGPT can understand and quote your content). You get a scored report that
groups every issue by how serious it is, so you know exactly what to fix first.

## Why it matters

You can't improve what you can't see. An audit turns your whole site into a
simple to-do list: a clear health score, the problems that are dragging it down,
and step-by-step fixes for each one. Run one now to get your baseline, then run
it again later to watch your score climb.

## How to run an audit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Audit page">
    Go to **Site audit** in the left sidebar. If you've never run one for this
    site, you'll see a **Run audit** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the check">
    Click **Run audit**. Spyro looks over your whole site - the button shows
    **Crawling...** and the page updates itself every few seconds. You don't need
    to reload or wait around.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read your report">
    When it's done, your scores and full report appear, along with a downloadable
    PDF. Run it again any time to refresh your scores and see what changed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You don't have to re-check your whole site to test a single fix. Use **URL
checks** to re-check one page on demand, or the **Re-check after fixes** button
on any page's report.
</Tip>

## What you'll see

At the top of the report, you always get a quick summary: two big score rings -
**Site Health** (your SEO score) and **AI Search Health** (your AI-readiness
score), each out of 100 - plus counts of **Errors**, **Warnings**, and
**Notices** found across your site.

Below the summary, you can switch between a few views:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pages" icon="layout-list">
    Every page Spyro checked, weakest first, with each page's scores and a
    health badge. Click any page to see its full detail.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full report" icon="file-text">
    Every issue found, grouped by how serious it is, with a short **how to fix**
    note for each one.
  </Card>
  <Card title="History" icon="trending-up">
    Your scores over time, what changed since your last check, and an estimate of
    how high your score could go once you fix things.
  </Card>
  <Card title="URL checks" icon="list-checks">
    Check a single page on demand - handy for spot-checking one URL after you've
    made edits.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Per-page detail

Click any page to open its own report. You'll see that page's scores, the issues
found on it, an **AI readiness** view, and a history of how it's changed. Each
issue comes with plain-language advice on how to fix it.

To learn how to read your scores, issues, fixes, and the PDF in depth, see
[Understand your reports](/product/understanding-reports).

## Re-check and the "Ask" helper

When you've made a change, you don't have to wait. Open the page and click
**Re-check after fixes** - Spyro re-checks just that page and shows you a
before-and-after so you can confirm the fix worked.

Not sure where to start? Click **Ask Spyro** to chat with a helper that knows
*your* audit. Ask things like *"Why is my AI score low?"* or *"What should I fix
first?"* and it answers using your own results. You can ask about your whole site
or about one page.

<Tip>
For any single issue, you can also ask Spyro to **suggest a fix**. It gives you a
short, do-this-now recommendation tailored to your site. It's advice for you to
apply - Spyro doesn't change your site for you.
</Tip>

## Tips

- Run your first audit right after setup to get your starting scores.
- Fix the **Errors** first - they have the biggest impact - then work through
  Warnings and Notices.
- Re-run the audit after a round of fixes to watch your score improve.
- Check the **History** view to see your progress over time.
