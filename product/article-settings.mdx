The **Article Planner** settings control how every article is written, styled,
illustrated, and published. They live under
`/{org}/{workspace}/article-planner/*` and are reached from the **Article Planner**
group in the sidebar.

<Note>
The matching entries under **Settings** (`/settings/blog`, `/settings/article-defaults`,
`/settings/text-generation`, `/settings/image-generation`, `/settings/integrations`)
are **redirect aliases** — they send you to these canonical Article Planner pages.
</Note>

## Blog & content

The **Blog & content** page holds your blog setup defaults — the high-level content
configuration captured at onboarding that frames what Spyro plans and writes.

## Article defaults

**Article defaults** sets the look and behavior of generated articles:

- **Article style** — the default writing style applied to new drafts.
- **Brand colors** — swatches derived from your site, used to tint images and
  accents.
- **Featured image dimensions** — the default size for the featured banner
  (1280×720 by default).
- **Auto-publish** — when enabled, finished articles publish to your connected
  integration automatically instead of waiting for a manual push.

## Text generation

The **Text generation** page shows the **voice** the AI Writer uses. Rather than a
generic "expert" tone, Spyro derives a voice profile from your own site —
a **summary**, **traits**, **audience**, and **formality**, labeled *derived from
your site* or *default voice*. Click **Regenerate** to re-derive it from your latest
content.

## Image generation

**Image generation** lets you pick the **featured-image style** every article uses.
Choose one of eight presets; each image is rendered in that style and tinted to your
brand colors:

| Preset | Look |
| --- | --- |
| **Flat vector** (default) | Clean flat illustration |
| **Hand-drawn ink** | Sketched ink line art |
| **Isometric 3D** | Isometric 3D render |
| **Editorial photographic** | Photographic, editorial |
| **Minimal abstract geometric** | Minimal geometric shapes |
| **Risograph** | Riso-print texture |
| **Paper cut** | Layered paper-cut style |
| **Technical blueprint** | Blueprint line schematic |

## Integrations

The **Integrations** page connects publishing destinations — **WordPress**,
**Shopify**, or any **webhook** URL. Each connection uses your workspace API key
(manage keys in [Settings → API](/product/settings)).

- Add a destination and Spyro can publish articles to it from the
  [Writer](/product/writer).
- Each card shows connection **status**, the connected URL, the reported plugin
  version (WordPress), and a **delivery history** of recent pushes (event, status
  code, duration).
