The dashboard is your home base for one website. It answers a single question -
*how is my site doing?* - across search rankings, AI visibility, and content.
Most cards also link straight to the tool behind them, so you can jump in with
one click.

At the top, you'll see your website name and two quick buttons: **Run audit**
and **Manage** (your workspace settings).

## Why use it

Instead of opening every tool to check on things, the dashboard pulls the
highlights into one screen. A quick look tells you whether your scores are going
up, which keywords are moving, and whether AI assistants are mentioning you. Use
it as your starting point each time you log in.

## What you'll see

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Your scores" icon="gauge">
    Your **SEO health** and **AI readiness** scores, each out of 100, with a small
    up-or-down trend once you've run more than one audit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI citation rate" icon="robot">
    How often AI assistants mention you when answering buyer questions, plus your
    **domain rating** (a measure of your site's authority).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Visibility over time" icon="chart-line">
    A chart of your scores across recent audits, so you can see the direction
    you're heading - not just today's number.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI engine citations" icon="quote-left">
    A bar for each AI assistant (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude) showing how
    often each one cites your site.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search performance" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Your Google clicks and impressions for the last 28 days, with the change
    versus the month before. Links to [Search Console](/product/search-console).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rank movers" icon="arrow-up-arrow-down">
    The keywords that moved the most since the last check - gains in green, drops
    in red. Links to the [rank tracker](/product/rank-tracker).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Recent AI mentions

When an AI assistant cites your site in an answer, the latest few show up as
cards here - each with the assistant, the date, how positive the mention was, and
a snippet of what it said. Tap a card to read the full mention, or choose
**See all** to open the [AI mentions feed](/product/ai-mentions).

## What to expect on your first login

A brand-new workspace starts mostly empty - and that's normal. Your scores show a
dash (`-`) and several panels invite you to get started. Everything fills in on
its own as Spyro finishes its work:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Your first audit completes">
    Your SEO and AI-readiness scores and the visibility chart appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your AI questions run">
    The AI citation rate and recent mentions begin to fill in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You connect Search Console">
    Your search performance card shows real clicks and impressions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You track keywords">
    The rank movers card starts showing where your keywords are moving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Right after setup, you may also see a status banner while Spyro looks over your
site for the first time. It disappears automatically once your workspace is
ready.
</Tip>
