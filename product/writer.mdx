The **AI writer** writes a full article for you. You choose a topic, and Spyro
researches it, writes the draft, and even adds images - so you start with a finished
piece instead of a blank page. From there you can polish it, check how well it's
optimized for search, and publish it straight to your website.

## Why use it

- **Go from idea to article in minutes.** Spyro does the research and writing for
  you.
- **Images included.** A matching banner image and in-article images are created
  automatically, styled to your brand.
- **Built for search.** Every draft is written to rank, and you get a clear SEO
  score so you know where it stands.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a topic">
    Open an idea from your [content calendar](/product/calendar), or send a topic
    from the [AI agent](/product/spyro-ai-agent), and tell Spyro to write it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let Spyro write it">
    Spyro researches the topic, writes the draft, and adds images. You'll see its
    progress as it works, and the editor opens automatically when the article is
    ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and polish">
    Review the draft in the editor. Change the wording, headings, and formatting,
    add or remove images, and fine-tune the title and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the SEO score">
    Look at the SEO score panel to see how well the article is optimized, then make
    quick improvements before publishing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish to your website">
    Send the finished article to your website with one click, or schedule it for
    later. You can also download the draft to use somewhere else.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to expect

While Spyro writes, you'll see it move through researching, drafting, and finishing
the images. This usually takes a short while - the editor opens on its own when
everything is done. If something gets stuck, a **Retry** button lets you start it
again without losing your work.

Once the draft is ready, you get a simple, familiar editor - much like a regular
word processor. You can type directly, format text, add tables and images, and see
your SEO score and target keyword alongside the article. When you're happy, publish
it to a connected website.

## Tips

- Set your brand voice, article style, and image look in
  [Content settings](/product/article-settings) before you write, so every article
  comes out on-brand.
- Connect your website first in [Integrations](/product/integrations) so you can
  publish without leaving the page.
- Use the SEO score as a guide - small edits to your title, description, and
  headings often give it a quick boost.
