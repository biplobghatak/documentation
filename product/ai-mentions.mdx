**AI mentions** is your front-row seat to what the AI assistants actually *say*.
It's a live feed of every answer ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity gave to
your [tracking prompts](/product/prompts) - so instead of just numbers, you see the
real words.

Where [AI visibility](/product/ai-visibility) tells you *how often* you're
mentioned and [Tracking prompts](/product/prompts) manages *what gets asked*, this
view shows you the *exact answers* - including the precise sentence where your
brand appears.

## What's in the feed

Each row is one AI answer from the last 30 days, newest first. For every answer you
can see:

- **Which assistant answered** - ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, or Perplexity.
- **When** the check ran.
- **The question** that prompted the answer.
- **Whether it mentioned you** - a green **Mentioned** badge when it named you.
- **The tone** - positive, neutral, or negative, for answers that mentioned you.
- **The snippet** - the exact excerpt of the answer, with your brand highlighted.

A summary at the top rolls up the same 30 days: how many answers there were, how
many mentioned you, and the overall positive / neutral / negative split.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI mentions">
    In the sidebar, open **AI mentions** (part of AI visibility).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan for the green badges">
    Look for **Mentioned** badges to see which answers actually named you, and on
    which assistants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the snippet">
    The highlighted snippet shows the exact sentence you appeared in, so you can
    judge *how* you were described - not just whether you were named.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch the negatives">
    Pay special attention to negative answers. Those are steering buyers away from
    you, and fixing them has the biggest payoff.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the full answer">
    Click any row to read the complete response, plus any sources the assistant
    linked to.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to expect

- The feed shows **every** answer in the window - mentioned or not - so you can see
  the full picture, even when an assistant didn't name you.
- Tone is only shown for answers that actually mentioned you. A very recent answer
  may briefly show as not yet labeled.
- Each answer links to the full response and its sources, so you can read it in
  full context.

## Tips

- This feed is the best place to understand *why* your
  [share of voice](/product/ai-visibility) moved. A drop usually traces back to a
  specific answer here - a rival the assistant started recommending, or a question
  where it stopped naming you.
- When you find a negative or missed mention, note the question, then improve the
  content that should be answering it.
