Spyro is built for teams. You invite people by email, choose what they can do, and
decide which of your websites they can work on. This page walks through the whole
thing.

## Why use it

Bring in writers, marketers, or clients so they can pitch in - without handing over
the keys to everything. You decide who manages the account and who just works on
the sites you assign them.

## Roles in plain terms

There are two simple kinds of people on your team:

- **Owners and admins** can manage everything - settings, billing, content, and the
  team itself, across every website.
- **Members** get access only to the websites you assign them. They work on those
  sites and nothing else.

<Note>
There is one **owner** per account. Only the owner can change your plan, manage
billing, or hand the account over to someone else.
</Note>

## Inviting teammates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your team settings">
    Go to **Settings** in the sidebar and find the team or members section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email and pick a role">
    Type a teammate's email and choose whether they can manage everything or just
    the sites you give them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Spyro emails them a link. You can also copy the link and share it yourself.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Invite someone to a **single website** when they only need to work on one site.
Invite them to the **whole account** when you want them across everything.
</Tip>

## Accepting an invite

If you're the person being invited, here's all it takes:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the link">
    Click the invite link in your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with the invited email">
    Use the **same email address the invite was sent to**. A forwarded link won't
    work for a different account. If you don't have a Spyro account yet, you'll
    create one first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start working">
    You're dropped straight into the workspace you've been given access to.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Invites expire after **14 days**. If a link has expired, just ask for a fresh one.
</Warning>

## Changing roles and removing people

- **Change a role** - pick a new role from the dropdown next to a teammate.
- **Remove someone from a website** - unassign them from just that site; they keep
  access to anything else they were given.
- **Remove someone from the account** - remove them entirely so they can't reach
  anything.
- **Cancel an invite** - revoke any invite that hasn't been accepted yet.

<Note>
The owner can't be removed or changed. Only owners and admins manage the team.
</Note>
